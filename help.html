Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, navigate to the course page and click the "Enroll" button. If the course is free, you'll be enrolled immediately. If it's a paid course, you'll be prompted to complete the payment process before enrollment.

How do I track my progress?

You can track your progress on the Dashboard page. It shows your enrolled courses, completion percentage, and upcoming deadlines. You can also view detailed progress for each course on the course page.

How do I get a certificate?

Certificates are awarded automatically upon successful completion of a course. You can view and download your certificates from the "Certificates" section in your profile. You can also share them directly to LinkedIn or other platforms.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Billing > Current Plan and click "Change Plan". From there, select "Cancel Subscription". Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
